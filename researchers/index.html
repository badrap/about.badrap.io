Badrap Security Researchers

Become a Badrap Security Researcher

Badrap changes the last mile in vulnerability coordination: we provide a channel so that you can directly tell about the problem to people that are affected. Individuals can easily check if their IP address is found within your data. You help individuals concretely and tangibly and ensure your hard work ultimately goes to a good cause.

Early adopters

We cheer for the security researchers, who share our vision of democratising the information sharing and joined early on to make their data available!
