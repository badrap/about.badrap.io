Security Startup Test Drive

Inspired by ScanABC Security Startup Test Drive we at Badrap would like to offer you a risk-free way to test drive life of a security startup entrepreneur.

What you'll get out of it

Experience similar to if you were working on your startup. You will get as much freedom and responsibility as you can take. You will also see what the founders see. You would be one of us.

What we look for

An outgoing, entrepreneur-minded person who currently lives in Oulu. You like to take an abstract goal, break it down and turn it into concrete actions. You are not afraid to ask for help when you need it. You've been around startups before, so you have some understanding about the laws that surround startups. Preferably you have also lived outside of Finland to help us better reach foreign markets.

What you would do

You would have an area of responsibility, but you would get to participate in all aspects of a startup. Funding, messaging, productisation, trial marketing, sales, and so forth. You would pair up with Jani, the CEO of the company. You would be a peer, not a subordinate.

... in practice

You would start with by updating our Cyber Hygiene for Employees -product with Jani and a few outsourced trial marketeers. Quite soon, you would be listing potential partners, end-customers and ways to reach them. You would track progress and collect feedback systematically so that we get proof-points on commercial viability.

Timeline

We would like you to start as soon as possible, 2019 is almost over! ;) We will evaluate the results of our 2019 plan in 2019-10 and adjust our plans for 2020 accordingly. At this point also you have a natural checkpoint for asssessing what you want to do next.

No strings attached

What happens after the test drive, is up to you and our success. We are not shy to offer a piece of the pie if it looks you can help us grow the pie. If you just want to get the experience, that is also fine.

Why are we doing this

At this phase of the company, we need people who are entrepreneur-minded. In other words, someone who naturally takes responsibility. This model also plays well into our strengths and weaknesses. We like to empower people, e.g. let them grow to fit bigger shoes. That means exposing people to as many aspects of the company as possible. Being transparent is our second nature. The flipside of this approach is that we suck at micro-managing people, so if you expect well-defined tasks and day-to-day instructions on what to do, you need to wait until we grow and hire managers.

Who are we

We are security startup veterans each with 10-20 years of experience on both building security products and companies who sell them. We'd love to tap into fresh blood and also share our experiences with you.
