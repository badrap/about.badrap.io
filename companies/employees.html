Cyber Hygiene for Employees provides an excellent opportunity for you to increase your employee security awareness. Your employees get to hunt security issues with a browser-based badrap.io service. They check their homes, relatives, schools and other places they care about. Empower your employees and make the more security aware!

Why work with us?

Badrap was founded be security veterans, who have served countries and critical infrastructure organisations for ages. We have witnessed how the Finnish approach of collecting and sharing security information with those in danger have helped organisations and individuals to stay safe. We've seen how other countries adapt that model. Now we want to make security information accessible to everyone, including you and your employees.
