Are you responsible of the security of systems, or the privacy of your users?


We have great news for you! GDPR’s 72-hour breach notification rule means that every organisation needs to be well prepared for an incident.

You can’t get on top of it after the fact. You know what that means? You will get to sleep better. Why? Because being well prepared, you have no doubt in your mind if you have covered all the angles.

We have a battle-hardened team of coordinators, legal experts and crisis communicators to make sure you will ace the GDPR’s 72h notificaiton responsibility. We have split the service for three parts.

Ready

Run a GDRP Data Breach Exercise with us. Live through the whole process and get templates and workflows that you can use if the worst happens.

Get set

Stay ready with us and be able to show it! We will keep up your capability and keep you prepared. To show off all the great work you’ve done with us, you will have a GDRP notification readiness status page summarising your readiness.

Go!

We hope you never need this service. But when if you do, you will be so glad you have it! In those crucial 72 hours our coordinators, legal advisors and crisis communicators have your back.
