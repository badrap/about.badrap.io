Delivering Security Information to the People

Society and individuals need safety to progress and prosper. We see security researchers and companies with information and ability to help and regular individuals needing help. Badrap is a place for them to meet and share security information.

Sharing is caring.
We connect people who care.

When Security Researchers discover information about vulnerabilities and data breaches, they want to warn you. When companies learn about a data breach, they need to warn you. You care about the privacy and security of yourself and your close ones. And you don't want to help attackers to attack others. This is why Badrap exists. We connect people and companies who care about each other, and let them warn each other.
