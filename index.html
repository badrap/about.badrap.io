Avoid Badrap

Bad guys hack normal people in order to hide their tracks online. They break into your devices or use them to bounce attacks towards others. They also break in to the services you use and steal personal data. We provide ways for security researchers and enterprises to alert you, if your device has been hacked or your data has been stolen.
