Bad guys hack normal people in order to hide their tracks online. They break into your devices or use them to bounce attacks towards others. They also break in to the services you use and steal personal data. Smart security researchers scan the net and find the weaknesses and stolen data. We provide ways for security researchers and enterprises to alert you, if your device has been hacked or your data has been stolen.

Vulnerable Devices

We have a localized solution to alert netizens (citizens on the net). We opened in Finland with a service called CheckYourNet. We attrack users with social media ads and content marketing. Netizens use the service to check if security researchers have reported issues in the IP-addresses they use to connect to the Intenret. If this is the case, netizen gets a human-understandable description of the problem, as well as remediation instructions. We also provide pointers to commercial help, if the user does can not fix the issues themself.
