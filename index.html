Sharing is caring.
We connect people who care.

When Security Researchers discover information about vulnerabilities and data breaches, they want to warn you and affected companies. When companies learn about a data breach, they need to warn you. You care about the privacy and security of yourself and your close ones. And you don't want to help attackers to attack others. This is why Badrap exists. We connect people and companies who care about each other, and let them warn each other.
