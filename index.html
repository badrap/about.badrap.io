Delivering Security Information to the People

Society and individuals need safety to progress and prosper. Regular people need help with their cyber security, and we see security experts with information and ability to help. We also see victims, who are attacked via hacked devices. We are creating a place for them to meet and warn each other. We opened in Finland and are working to go global.

Security Researchers and individuals

Sharing is caring. We want to connect people who care.

When Security Researchers discover information about vulnerabilities and data breaches, they want to warn you and affected companies. When companies learn hackers stole your private information, they need to warn you. You care about the privacy and security of yourself and your close ones. Moreover, you don't want to help attackers to attack others. All this is why Badrap exists. We connect people and companies who care about each other and let them warn each other.
