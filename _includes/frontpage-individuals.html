Security Researchers Can Warn You via Badrap

When security researchers scan the net, they find tons of issues. Vulnerable home routers, webcams with default passwords, and so forth. But they usually can't tell the owners of the vulnerable devices, so they can't warn you. They upload their findings to Badrap to let you check if your IP-addresses were on their list. If that is the case, you will get instructions on how to remedy the situation. You don't need to sign up to our service - but if you do, Badrap can warn you about new findings via email.

Has my data leaked?

Bad guys hack Internet-services to steal private information. Emails, passwords, credit card numbers and so on. In Europe, they are compelled by regulation to notify your personal information has leaked. In U.S, companies get gigantic fines and end up doing settlements when they don't act diligently in the data breaches. We help companies to notify you when they must. We provide them the means to help you to understand what happened.
